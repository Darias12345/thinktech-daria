ABOUT US

For the first time, Landmark Communications and Technologies introduce a conference which focuses entirely on technology as the field of the future. The "Think Tech" conference is focused on young people and the main goal is to convey a simple message, which I believe is very current. In summary, the call of "Think Tech" is: "Do not leave Albania, because even in the absence of initiatives of relevant institutions,

our country still offers many opportunities in the field of technology." The structure of "Think Tech" is divided into two main parts: The first relates to domestic and foreign enterprises. The second part of the meeting focuses on studies, training, advice and assistance that international organizations that operate in our market, offer to Albanian youth.
